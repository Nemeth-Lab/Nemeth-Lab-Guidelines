# Safety Guidelines

## Personal Protective Equipment (PPE):

1.	Wear appropriate PPE, including lab coats, safety goggles, gloves, and closed-toe shoes, when working in the lab
2.		Know the location of emergency showers, eye wash stations, fire extinguishers, and first aid kits.

## Handling Chemicals and Hazardous Materials:

1. Read and understand Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) for all chemicals before use.
2. Use fume hoods when working with volatile or noxious chemicals.
3. Dispose of hazardous waste properly according to [FAU EH&S protocols](https://www.fau.edu/ehs/safety/safety-manuals/).


## Equipment Safety:
1.	Follow equipment operation instructions and guidelines.
2.		Report any malfunctioning or damaged equipment immediately.

## Emergency Procedures:
1. Familiarize yourself with emergency evacuation routes and procedures.
2. In the event of a spill, follow spill cleanup procedures as outlined in the lab.
