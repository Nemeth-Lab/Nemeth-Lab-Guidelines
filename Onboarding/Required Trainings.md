### Navigating Skillsoft

1. Click on **EHS Safety Programs and Services**.
2. Select **‚ÄúRequest EH&S Training‚Äù**.
3. Choose the **OIT ticket link** and add needed courses to the description or attach a file with the list.
4. Within **2 days**, you will receive authorization to complete the courses.
5. The courses should populate in your **Percipio Training** link.

To launch a course:

* Click on the course name.
* A new page will open with a **Launch** button.
* Click **Launch** to begin the training video.

**Trouble logging into Skillsoft?**
Email **[security-training@fau.edu](mailto:security-training@fau.edu)** with:

* Full name
* FAU User ID
* Department *(Biological Sciences, Psychology, or Complex Systems and Brain Sciences)*

You must have a valid FAU email and be an active student or employee.
