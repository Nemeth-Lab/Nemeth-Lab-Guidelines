
# Minors Working at FAU

### Working with Minors and Non-FAU Students

The Office of Undergraduate Research and Inquiry (OURI) provides resources for faculty working with students under age 18. Faculty should review:

* FAU Children in the Workplace Policy
* FAU Volunteer Policy
* FAU EH&S Policy on Minors in Research Laboratories or Animal Facilities

See the Office of Leadership Education and Development’s **Volunteer Policies** page for additional guidance.

---

### Faculty Requirements

Faculty working with minors must:

* Initiate the **EH&S Minors in the Lab Proposal**
* Complete the **Child Labor Law Certification** form
* Complete the **Minor Certification** form
* Send both forms to **[EMPL@fau.edu](mailto:EMPL@fau.edu)**
* Complete a **background check** with Human Resources **before** initiating the Minors in the Lab Proposal (not required for FAUHS students)

---

### Determining Volunteer Status

**Is the student receiving course credit or financial compensation?**

#### YES

* Student is **not** considered a volunteer
* FAU Volunteer Registration form **not required**
* Student and mentor must initiate the **EH&S Minors in the Lab Proposal**

#### NO

* Student **is** considered a volunteer
* Student must be categorized as **Category 1** or **Category 2** volunteer
* Student and parent/guardian must complete the **Parent/Legal Guardian Consent for Criminal Background Check**
* Submit forms to **[EMPL@fau.edu](mailto:EMPL@fau.edu)**
* Background check fee: **approximately $20–$65**

---

### Category 1 Volunteer Requirements

Student and parent/guardian must complete:

* FAU Volunteer Registration Form
* Volunteer Waiver and Release Form
* Parental/Guardian Authorization for Treatment of Minors Form
* Background Check Parental Consent Form

Forms should be submitted to:

* **[EMPL@fau.edu](mailto:EMPL@fau.edu)** *(background check forms)*
* **[leadandserve@fau.edu](mailto:leadandserve@fau.edu)** *(volunteer forms)*

> The department hosting the student must maintain a copy of the **Authorization for Treatment of Minors** form.

---

### Category 2 Volunteer Requirements

Student and parent/guardian must complete:

* Volunteer Waiver and Release Form
* Parental/Guardian Authorization for Treatment of Minors Form

Submit forms to **[leadandserve@fau.edu](mailto:leadandserve@fau.edu)**.

---

### Environmental Health and Safety Minors in the Lab Proposal

Faculty must:

* Submit the **Minors in the Lab Proposal** to EH&S **at least 2 weeks before** the project start date
* Ensure student and guardian complete **Parental Informed Consent and Permission** forms
* Submit documents to **[ehs@fau.edu](mailto:ehs@fau.edu)**
* If working with animals, review the **IACUC Policy and Procedure for Children in Animal Facilities**
* Ensure the student completes all required workplace trainings as advised by EH&S
